Robotic Drummer and Robotic Guitarist

Designing and Manufacturing a Robotic Band from Scratch

Overview

I love to work on projects that combine my interests. So as a musician and an engineer, I was a founding member and help lead the Robotic Band project in the Northwestern University Robotics Club

For this project, in 2024-2025 I helped design and manufacture parts for the Robotic Drummer, including designing the intial torso mechanism and 3D printing parts for the drummer units. In the 2025-2026 school year, I lead a sub-team to design a plucking mechanism for a Robotic Guitarist.
