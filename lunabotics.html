Lunabotics Excavation Rover

For NASA's Lunabotics competition, rover design and manufacturing.

Overview

The Lunabotics competition is an annual NASA-sponsored competition where university teams design and build excavation rovers for lunar exploration. I'm part of the Northwestern inaugural team competing in the 2026 Competition.

I help manufacturing efforts, using milling, 3D printing, and waterjetting to create mechanisms for the rover, including parts for the wheels, and the excavation arm.
